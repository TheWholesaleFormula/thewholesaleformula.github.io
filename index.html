What Is The Wholesale Formula?

The Wholesale Formula (TWF) is a step-by-step training program designed to help individuals build and scale a profitable online business by leveraging wholesale partnerships and selling products through Amazon’s Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) platform. Created by Dan Meadors and Dylan Frost, the program teaches a unique approach called Reverse Sourcing Wholesale, which focuses on partnering directly with brand owners to sell their already successful products on Amazon.

Rather than creating private label products or engaging in retail arbitrage, TWF emphasizes identifying products that are already performing well on Amazon. The strategy involves reaching out to brand owners or manufacturers, securing wholesale agreements, and adding value to their brand by optimizing their Amazon presence. This can include improving product listings, enhancing marketing, and managing reviews.

TWF is highly scalable and designed to be lower risk because it avoids creating new products or testing unproven markets. By focusing on data-driven decisions, such as analyzing competition and sales metrics, users can predict profitability before investing in inventory. The program is especially appealing to entrepreneurs seeking a streamlined, systemized approach to eCommerce, with the added benefit of Amazon FBA handling storage, shipping, and customer service.

The Wholesale Formula aims to empower users to build sustainable, passive income businesses through proven, replicable processes.

How Does The Wholesale Formula Help You Make Money?

The Wholesale Formula (TWF) helps you make money by teaching you how to create a profitable wholesale e-commerce business using Amazon Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA). The program leverages a proven method called Reverse Sourcing Wholesale, which allows you to partner directly with established brands to sell their products on Amazon.

How It Works:

1. Identify High-Performing Products: TWF teaches you to analyze Amazon sales data to find products already in demand. These products have a consistent sales history and a loyal customer base, reducing the risk of unsold inventory.

2. Form Wholesale Partnerships: Instead of buying from large distributors, you approach brand owners or manufacturers directly. By offering value-added services—such as optimizing product listings, managing inventory, or handling customer reviews—you establish mutually beneficial relationships.

3. Buy in Bulk at Wholesale Prices: Once you secure a deal, you purchase inventory at a discounted rate and ship it to Amazon’s warehouses.

4. Leverage Amazon FBA: Amazon handles storage, shipping, and customer service, saving you time and allowing you to focus on scaling your business.

By working with established products and outsourcing logistics, TWF offers a scalable and low-risk way to generate passive income. Its data-driven approach ensures profitability, while its focus on collaboration builds long-term business partnerships.

What Is Reverse Source Planning?

Reverse Source Planning, as the name suggests, is a method of source planning where things are done in reverse order. In usual source planning, a middleman is involved between you and the seller of the product. But in Reverse Source Planning, the middlemen are eliminated and you will be the one who will collect the product from the seller at a wholesale price. After this, you sell the product on Amazon. Here, you will be able to get the products at a cheaper price while maintaining its highest standards.

Now that you have an idea of what Reverse Source Planning is, let’s move to the working mechanism of The Wholesale Formula program. There are three steps involved in its working and they are the following:
